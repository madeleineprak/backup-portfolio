About

I am a Full Stack Web Developer with customer based experience ranging from restaurant service to GIS Analyst work at a transportation planning consultant company. Both worlds helped me ensure end-users are met with high quality products and positive experiences.

I received a Bachelors in Geographic Information Systems (GIS), minor in Informatics and most recently a certificate in Full Stack Web Development all through the University of Washington, Seattle. It has led me to a strong knowledge of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, several frameworks and backend. Through these different perspectives, I have gained communication, problem-solving, and adaptability skills that have moulded myself to the developer I am today.

I have worked on many team projects including building a data analysis dashboard which increased my knowledge of synergy and working with individuals of different backgrounds. Throughout these experiences, I have found a passion in seeing the results of solving real-world problems with complex code.
